Instructions

  • 1. Press any key to start the game. The level will begin at 1.
  • 2. Remember the sequence of colours shown by the game.
  • 3. Click the buttons in the same order as the sequence.
  • 4. If you make a mistake, the game ends. Press any key to restart.
  • 5. Try to reach the highest level and beat your high score!
  • Enjoy playing Simon!
